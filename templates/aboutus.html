About Us

What NomNom Bites Is

NomNom Bites is a recipe maker and tracker that recommends meals based on ingredients you already have. We eliminate the frustration of finding recipes only to discover you're missing key items.


Our intelligent recommendation system lets you simply input your available ingredients, and we'll suggest creative meal ideas that match. This saves time, reduces food waste, and inspires culinary creativity.


Beyond recommendations, we offer a complete recipe management system where you can explore popular dishes, save favorites, and share your creations. Our integrated grocery shopping makes ordering missing ingredients seamless.
